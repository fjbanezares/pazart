Engraving Technique:

After her fine arts studies, the Artist studied to be a master in this complex technique where you have to carve the wood or the metal in order to make the base to manufacture the final piece of art.

This is very close to the editorial printing, from one base you can print a huge number of copies.

In the case of engraving, after you have done the base, you can play with the color. Even you can let it be in black and white letting the details of the carve of the wood be more relevant.

  • Om Mani Padme Hum

    The meaning of this mantra is: with 'OM' we are calling Avalokiteshvara, 'MANI' means the precious jewel of enlightenment, 'PAME' means liberation and 'HUM' means bestow. Together, the meaning is: 'O Avalokiteshvara, please bestow the precious jewel of enlightenment to liberate all living beings'. Through the recitation of this mantra we train in the compassionate mind of bodhichitta.
