A life of inspiration:

As an Artist Paz Arés knows that Creativity arises from inside. Nonetheless She has devoted all her life to study and inspiration in order to boost the inner creativity process and make it relevant with the social issues.

In order to that Paz Ares Osset has four university degrees apart from tens of other minor studies. She has also worked during 45 years as a public servant of Spanish government as a teacher, trying always to innovate in the rotten Spanish traditional methods of education.
